Our Story

About Virtus Transport

We are on a mission to transform last-mile logistics — making every delivery faster, smarter, and more sustainable for businesses and communities worldwide.

CONNECTING THE LAST MILE
Our Mission

Making last-mile delivery efficient, sustainable, and stress-free

Every day, millions of parcels travel the final stretch to doorsteps around the world. That last mile is the hardest, the most expensive, and the most polluting leg of the journey. We believe technology can change that.

Efficient

Cut delivery times and costs with AI-powered route optimization that adapts in real time.

Stress-Free

Give dispatchers, drivers, and customers full visibility and control — no more guesswork.

Sustainable

Fewer miles driven means lower emissions. Our platform helps fleets reduce their carbon footprint by up to 30%.

Our Journey

From a London startup to a global platform

What started as a simple idea to fix broken delivery routes has grown into a platform trusted by thousands of companies across the world.

2019

Founded in London

Two logistics veterans saw how inefficient last-mile routing had become. They built the first prototype in a Shoreditch co-working space.

2020

First 100 Customers

The pandemic accelerated demand for delivery. Virtus helped courier companies scale operations when they needed it most.

2021

Series A Funding

Raised a significant round led by top-tier investors, allowing us to triple the engineering team and accelerate product development.

2022

Expanded to 15 Countries

Opened offices in Berlin and Dubai. Launched multi-language support and region-specific compliance features.

2023

500M+ Deliveries Milestone

Half a billion deliveries optimized through the Virtus platform. Launched the Driver App and real-time analytics dashboard.

2024

AI Route Engine Launched

Introduced our next-generation AI routing engine, cutting average delivery times by 22% and fuel costs by 18% for customers.

2025

1B+ Deliveries, 30+ Countries

One billion deliveries and counting. Operating across 30+ countries with 200+ employees and a relentless focus on what comes next.

Our Values

What drives us every day
